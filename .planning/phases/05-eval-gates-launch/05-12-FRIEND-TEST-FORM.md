## Form title

**Joe's resume chat agent — quick feedback (5 min)**

## Form description (intro shown to testers above the questions)

```
Thanks for testing this!

This is the chat agent attached to Joe Dollinger's resume — recruiters
scan the QR code on the paper resume (or click the URL) and land here.
Goal: in 5 min, walk away with a specific impression of Joe and what
he's done, without any fabrication.

Try it for ~5 min:
https://joe-dollinger-chat.com

Suggestions if you don't know what to ask:
  • Ask about a project or role on his resume
  • Tell it you work at [some company] — see what it does with that
  • Throw a curveball or test how it handles "I don't know"
  • Try to break it (politely)

Then come back here and answer 5 quick questions. Anonymous; takes ~2 min.
```

   - Type: Paragraph
   - Required: No

5. **Open feedback.**
   - Type: Paragraph
   - Required: No
